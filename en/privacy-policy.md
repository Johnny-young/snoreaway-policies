# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 15, 2026**

SnoreAway ("we", "us") operates the SnoreAway mobile app. This page explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data.

## Data We Collect

- **Account**: Email address or Apple ID (unique identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email).
- **Profile**: Display name, gender, birth year (optional).
- **Questionnaire**: Your answers to the snoring assessment.
- **Wellness Data**: Check-in records, sleep observations, and cycle feedback.
- **Feedback**: Bug reports and support messages.
- **Automatically**: Subscription status and anonymous app user ID (via RevenueCat).

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Microphone or audio recordings
- Precise location (GPS)
- Contacts, photos, or media
- Data from children under the applicable legal age

## How We Use Your Data

- Generate personalized wellness analysis and plans
- Track your progress through wellness cycles
- Manage and restore your subscription
- Fix crashes and improve stability
- Respond to your feedback and support requests

## Health Disclaimer

SnoreAway provides wellness and lifestyle suggestions only. It does **not** provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or prescription. If you experience severe or persistent snoring, suspected sleep apnea, or other health concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Supabase | Database and authentication |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management (processes subscription status and anonymous user ID) |
| Third-party AI service | Generating wellness analysis and plans |
| Apple | Sign in with Apple |

We share only the minimum data necessary. We do not sell your data.

## AI Processing

We use a third-party AI service to generate wellness analysis and plans. Only anonymized information (no names or email addresses) is sent. Per the AI provider's policy, your data is not used to train their models.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence. We take reasonable steps to ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

## Your Rights

- **Access**: Contact us to request a copy of your data.
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate.
- **Withdraw Consent**: Delete your account at any time.

## Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Auth tokens are stored in iOS Keychain. Database access uses row-level security.

## Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Deleting your account immediately and permanently removes all your data from our servers.

## Legal Basis (GDPR)

For EEA users, we process data under: your consent, contractual necessity, and legitimate interest.

## Children

We do not knowingly collect data from children below the applicable legal age in their country.
